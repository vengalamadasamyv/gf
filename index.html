Our Story

Crafting Visual Legacies Since 1992

Born from a deep passion for visual storytelling, Global Fotos has been Tamil Nadu's premier photography studio for over three decades. We were among the first generation of professionals to master the art of visual communication in the region.

Our philosophy is simple: every moment holds a universe of emotion. Our lens is the instrument that preserves those universes — wedding ceremonies, baby's first smiles, maternity glow, outdoor adventures — each story told with uncompromising artistry.

  • Award-winning team
  • State-of-the-art studio
  • Premium editing suite
  • Same-day previews
  • Physical & digital delivery
  • Custom print packages
